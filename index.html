My journey started writing code in 2006 — but the most impactful chapters came when I stepped into leadership. Over 20 years I've moved from engineer to architect to team lead across startups, agencies, scale-ups, and global brands.

Today I lead the Digital Operations team at Nespresso, managing Frontend, SEO, QA, and Accessibility engineering. My technical depth lets me make decisions that are both strategically sound and architecturally solid — from defining a Design System that scaled campaign output from 7 to 42 per year, to shaping team roadmaps and mentoring engineers.

I believe great technology leaders don't just delegate — they understand the code, set the standards, and align every technical decision with business outcomes.
